A little about me...

I've been involved with interactive technology for most of my life. I enjoy taking things apart to figure out how they work. I'm an excellent researcher and self-learner. I'm currently participating in a 'batch' at the Recurse Center to sharpen my skills and be inspired by a cohort of talented programmers. Previously I taught at Universities and worked as a Lighting and Sound Designer and Production Manager. I'd love to work on interesting things and make the world a better place. I am especially interested in environmental conservation, education, and access to essential resources (food, shelter, water, healthcare).
